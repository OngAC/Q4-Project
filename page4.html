CELEBRITY

Kim Da-Mi

김다미




Kim Da-Mi, a South Korean actress renowned for her exceptional acting skills and captivating appearance, was recently featured by Bottega Veneta. These photos beautifully showcase her allure, perfectly aligned with Bottega Veneta's aesthetic.

Da-Mi gained national and international recognition through her roles in popular dramas such as "Our Beloved Summer," "The Witch," and "Itaewon Class." She has received widespread acclaim for her ability to portray characters with both innocence and fierceness, earning multiple awards for her performances.

Her popularity continues to soar, with a growing fanbase eagerly anticipating her future projects in both movies and dramas.
